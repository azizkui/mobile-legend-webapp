Thousand Pounder

Cooldown: 10
Mana Cost: 70
Akai jumps towards a designated location. Upon landing, Akai deals 300(+65%Total Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage to enemy units, while expercieincing a slow effect for 2 seconds. Akai deals bonus damage to marked enemies and causes a stun effect for 1 seconds.

Blender

Cooldown: 6.5
Cooldown: 6.5
Mana Cost: 65
Akai fires a frog shaped shockwave in the designated direction that explodes on contact with enemies or once it reaches maximum range, dealing 350(+135%Extra Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage and marking them for 4 seconds. His basic attacks on marked targets deal extra 8% of Akai's maximum health as physical damage.

Hurricane Dance

Cooldown: 35.5
Mana Cost: 120
Akai removes all CC effects from him and spins like a tornado for 3.5 seconds. Each collision knocks back the target and deals 180(+165%Extra Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage (damage decays against the same target). During the time, Akai becomes immune to all CC effects and gains movement speed by 30%.
